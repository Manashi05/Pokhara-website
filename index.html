POKHARA

Pokhara is a picturesque city in Nepal, nestled between the Annapurna mountain range and serene
lakes, known for its natural beauty, adventure tourism, and vibrant culture.

Places to visit

Phewa Lake

Phewa Lake, located in Pokhara, is a tranquil freshwater lake surrounded by lush hills and offering stunning reflections of the Annapurna and Machapuchare mountain peaks. It is a popular destination for boating, lakeside relaxation, and visiting the Tal Barahi Temple situated on an island in the middle of the lake.

Begnas Lake

Begnas Lake, located in Pokhara, is a tranquil freshwater lake surrounded by lush hills and offers a peaceful escape from the city's bustle. Known for its serene beauty, it's a popular spot for boating, fishing, and enjoying stunning views of the Annapurna and Manaslu mountain ranges.

Devi's Fall

Devi's Fall, also known as Patale Chhango, is a stunning waterfall in Pokhara where the Pardi Khola river disappears into an underground tunnel. It's a popular tourist attraction with a mysterious origin, named after a tragic accident in which a Swiss woman named Devi drowned in the fall.

Sarangkot

Sarangkot, located near Pokhara, is a popular hilltop destination offering breathtaking sunrise views over the Annapurna and Dhaulagiri mountain ranges. It is also renowned for its paragliding opportunities, providing a thrilling aerial perspective of the city and surrounding landscapes.

Mountains

Mt. Macchapuchre

Mount Machapuchare, often called the "Fish Tail" mountain due to its unique twin-peaked shape, is a sacred and unclimbed peak in the Annapurna range near Pokhara, Nepal. Revered for its striking beauty, it is considered holy by locals, and climbing to its summit is forbidden out of respect for its spiritual significance.

Mt. Annapurna

Mount Annapurna, part of the Annapurna massif, is one of the tallest peaks in the Himalayas, located near Pokhara, Nepal. Renowned for its challenging trekking routes and stunning scenery, it attracts adventurers and nature lovers from around the world.
