About Pokhara

Pokhara, the second-largest city in Nepal, is a picturesque destination nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas. Known for its natural beauty, it serves as the gateway to the Annapurna mountain range, attracting trekkers and adventurers from around the world. The city’s charm lies in its tranquil lakes, lush green valleys, and stunning views of snow-capped peaks, including Machapuchare and Annapurna.


Pokhara also boasts a rich cultural heritage, with numerous temples, stupas, and a blend of local traditions. The World Peace Pagoda, perched on a hill, provides panoramic views of the city, lake, and surrounding mountains. Its peaceful atmosphere complements Pokhara’s reputation as a place of relaxation and spiritual retreat.


Whether for adventure, relaxation, or cultural exploration, Pokhara stands as a must-visit destination, blending natural beauty and a sense of peace with the vibrancy of local life.
